August 8, 2020

My new song "The Theory of Love" is out on all major platforms!

After weeks waiting for the final mixing and mastering my song "The Theory of Love" is available
to stream and download/buy. This particular song of mine is actually pretty old, as I wrote it more than 3 years ago but
never actually got around to arrange it before of April this year.
I am very happy about how it sounds with a full band and I hope you will like it too!
Please let me know what you think of it!

finding musicians to play with etc...
